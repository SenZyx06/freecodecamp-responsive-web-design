The life and Legacy

Who is he?

José Rizal (1861-1896) was a national hero of the Philippines and the first Asian nationalist. He expressed the growing national consciousness of many Filipinos who opposed Spanish colonial tyranny and aspired to attain democratic rights.

A list of works by Jose Rizal:

  • 1884 - Toast to Juan Luna and Felix Hidalgo, speech given at Restaurante Ingels, Madrid
  • 1887 - Noli Me Tángere, his first novel
  • 1889 - Sa Mga Kababaihang Taga Malolos, an essay
  • 1889 - Sucesos de las Islas Filipinas, an essay, Annotations to Antonio de Moragas
  • 1889-90 - Filipinas dentro de cien años, an essay
  • 1890 - La Indolencia de los Filipinos, an essay
  • Undated - Makamisa, an unfinished novel
  • Undated - The Friars and the Filipinos
  • Undated - The Diaries of Jose Rizal
